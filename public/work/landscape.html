Adventures
I started taking photos to document my adventures when I first moved to Colorado. It is such a beautiful state with so many places to explore from city rooftops, campus labyrinths, to mountain tops and desert. I am always open to new adventures that will push my limits and give me an experience that is new.
My most recent adventure was spending 22 hours in the city of Frankfurt during an extended layover. I decided to not spend the day/night in a hotel but to explore as much as possible of the city. I spent the night roaming the city and looking for the best view of the skyline. I then took the train to the neighboring towns and explored till the train stopped at 3 AM.
